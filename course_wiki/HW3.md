Complete your PCB, error check it, and generate the gerber files. Upload your design to git in a folder called HW3, and submit your .zip file with the gerbers on CANVAS.

Watch the following video as an example:

[Youtube libraries video](https://youtu.be/8nQviJYJ7OE)

Make sure your CONN_0x header pins are at the following locations:
![](https://github.com/ndm736/ME433_2019/blob/master/eagle/pcb_placement.png)

so that another PCB, with lots of breadboard-like space, will fit on top:
![](https://github.com/ndm736/ME433_2019/blob/master/eagle/pcb_shield.png)

Your final .sch should look something like:
![](https://github.com/ndm736/ME433_2019/blob/master/eagle/schematic.png)

and your final .brd should look something like:
![](https://github.com/ndm736/ME433_2019/blob/master/eagle/pcb.png)
